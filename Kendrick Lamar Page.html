This entire song alludes to the Book of Revelations in the Bible stating that if the human race continues to sin, judgment day will happen.
According to Revelations, 7 seals will be broken by a lamb, presumably the Lamb of God, and right after, 7 trumpets will play one by one.
Each trumpet represents a gruesome stage, becoming worse than the previous. Three monsters then come to cause even more destruction and the
event ends with 7 bowls being poured onto the earth, causing all sorts of catastrophe. Eventually, all dangers are sealed and Jesus rules
for 1,000 years. A thousand years pass and Satan and the others are released, only to be casted by fire and finally rid of. After this, a
place called "The New Jerusalem" will be made, which is a solid gold cube vasting many many miles. God, Jesus, all of the good humans, and
the multiple eyed angels will live in harmony.

In the lyrics themselves, Kendrick hopes that he'll be able to enter Heaven because he is the "valedictorian" of the rap game; the greatest.
Next, he was a straight A student. Finally, he fears God for the unimaginative power He holds, which is common with many other people who
believe in God.
